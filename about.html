About

Staff

Judy - For over 15 years, Judith Wildfire has provided technical assistance in self-evaluation to child welfare and human service programs. Judy has extensive experience with statistical analysis using survey data, existing administrative data files, vital statistical records such as birth certificate data, and program files from WIC and Medicaid programs. Judy holds a masters degree in Public Administration and an MPH in biostatistics, both from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

Tim - Timothy Wildfire has worked in state and county mental health agencies for over 20 years. He has developed information systems to track costs and helped agency staff implement outcome-based performanice monitoring systems. Tim holds a M.S.W. from Rutgers University.

Ben - Ben Wildfire has been working as a data and policy analyst for Wildfire Associates for the past 2 years after experience working in the legal department of New York Administration for Children's Services. He holds a J.D. from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.
